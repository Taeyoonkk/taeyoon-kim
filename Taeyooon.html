How to make 'Kimchi rice cake'?


Ingredients

Kimchi

2 bottle of flour

A bottle of water

1 egg

A bottle of water

topings

Directions

Put all ingredients into a bowl then mix it together. you add more water it become crunchy. If you add more flour it become like a cake.

Add oil to a frying pan. The intensity of the fire is medium. After 1 minuites put the mixture. If one side looks like the picture, it turns upside down.

When another side looks like the picture too, put it on a plate.

It's so delicious! Please make it if you have Kimch.
